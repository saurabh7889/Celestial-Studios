Saurabh Kumar

Founder • Celestial Studios

S aurabh Kumar is the founder of Celestial Studios, a digital-first creative agency focused on helping businesses establish a strong online presence through modern websites, branding, digital marketing, and AI-powered solutions.

Currently pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering (AI & ML), Saurabh started Celestial Studios with a vision to combine technology, creativity, and automation to help businesses grow in a rapidly changing digital world.

His passion lies in web development, artificial intelligence, user experience design, and digital strategy. Rather than offering one-size-fits-all services, he focuses on building practical solutions that help businesses attract customers, improve credibility, and scale efficiently.

Through Celestial Studios, Saurabh aims to create a future-ready agency where businesses can access modern technology, smart automation, and creative digital solutions under one roof.

"Technology should make growth easier. My goal is to help businesses leverage websites, branding, marketing, and AI to create meaningful digital growth."
